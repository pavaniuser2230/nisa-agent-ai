## 🚀 How to Use AGENT NISA AI

### 1. Choose Your Task Type
Select what kind of help you need (career, study, content, etc.)

### 2. Select Output Format
Choose how you want the results (email, list, notes, plan, etc.)

### 3. Describe Your Request
Be specific about what you need:

**Good Example:**
> "Find AI/ML internships for beginners in India with remote options. Generate professional application messages for each opportunity that highlight my Python and data analysis skills."

**Another Good Example:**
> "Summarize the key concepts from my machine learning research paper and create study notes with practical examples."

### 4. Add Context (Optional)
Include any additional information:
- Your skills and experience
- Specific preferences
- Deadlines or timeline
- Location preferences

### 5. Receive Comprehensive Results
Get a complete package with:
- ✅ Research findings
- ✅ Generated content
- ✅ Action steps
- ✅ Templates ready to use
- ✅ Additional resources
- ✅ Implementation guidance

## ⚖️ Ethical Guidelines

AGENT NISA AI operates with strict ethical standards:

### ✅ We DO:
- Generate drafts for you to review and customize
- Respect platform policies and terms of service
- Protect your privacy
- Provide transparent reasoning
- Cite sources and verify information
- Emphasize learning and skill development

### ❌ We DON'T:
- Send messages or applications without your review
- Support spam or mass messaging
- Impersonate users
- Share personal information
- Encourage shortcuts over learning
- Violate platform rules

### ⚠️ Important Reminders:
- **Always review and customize** generated content before using
- **Personalize templates** with your information
- **Verify information** before relying on it
- **Focus on learning** - AI is a tool to help you succeed, not a replacement for effort
