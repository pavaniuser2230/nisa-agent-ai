// System Architecture

How NISA AI Agent Works

Six specialized AI agents collaborate in a pipeline to deliver personalized student assistance — from profile collection to final output delivery.

01 / COLLECT
📋
Student Profile Collection
NISA begins by collecting your academic background, skills, interests, and goals to build a comprehensive student profile.
02 / DISCOVER
🌐
Opportunity Discovery
A multi-agent orchestrator deploys worker agents to scan internships, hackathons, scholarships, jobs, and courses across hundreds of sources.
03 / EVALUATE
⚖️
Match Evaluation Loop
Each opportunity is scored against your profile. Missing skills are identified and learning resources are recommended to close the gap.
04 / ROUTE
🔀
NISA Router
The intelligent router determines which tasks to perform — career guidance, document generation, or research summarization.
05 / GENERATE
✨
Asset Generation Engine
Personalized resumes, cover letters, project documentation, and presentation slides are generated tailored to each opportunity.
06 / DELIVER
📬
Final Compilation & Notification
All results are compiled into a clean final report and delivered, ready for you to act on your best-matched opportunities.
